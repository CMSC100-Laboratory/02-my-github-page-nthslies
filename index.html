Projects Made

Another Day: An Idle Game

A game developed for CMSC 22, where players travel to places to get different items depending on their rarity, such as common, rare, ultra rare, and legendary. The system supports multiple player accounts but can only play one at a time when they are logged in. Each player can view their information, claim daily rewards, and manage their own inventory.

User Management System

An exercise developed for CMSC 22, where it allows users to create, delete, and log in to an account. It also displays the entries available and different information about the user, such as their email address, first name, middle name, last name, and their user type, like if they’re a student or a professor. Only when the entered password matches the saved password can they log in.

City Pollution Reduction Plan

A Shiny app developed for CMSC 150, where it helps users to select pollution mitigation projects and calculate an optimal plan to meet pollutant reduction targets at minimum cost.

Indak: A Rhythm Game

A game I developed togeher with my friends, wherein on an island called Panay lived a ‘babaylan’ called Carmela, living peacefully with her only daughter, Josefina. Until one day, she found out that she has a sickness that any normal person cannot cure. Due to this and her fear of her daughter being left alone, she made a deal with a devil wherein, upon her daughter’s coming of age, her powers will transfer to the devil.
